Synchronous Machines

Operation Characteristics

The frequency-speed relation of a synchronous generator is given by,

f=pn120

The RMS value of the generated voltage Ea is given by,

Ea=π2Nϕf=4.44Nϕf,ϕ=BA

Equivalent Circuit

Va=Ea−Ia(Ra+Xsj)

where,

  • Ra is armature resistance
  • Xs is synchronous reactance
  • δ is the power angle of the synchronous generator

Power

The complex power of a synchronous generator is given by,

S=3VaIa∗

Neglecting Ra, the active and reactive power are given by,
