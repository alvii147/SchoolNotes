Time Series Components

A time series can be broken into 4 components:

  • Trend, Tt
  • Cyclical, Ct
  • Seasonal, St
  • Irregular, It
Xt=Tt+Ct+St+It

Additional Components

  • The very low frequency component: Tt
  • The medium low frequency component: Ct
  • The medium high frequency component: St
  • The very high frequency component: It
  • The low frequency component: Tt+Ct
  • The high frequency component St+It
